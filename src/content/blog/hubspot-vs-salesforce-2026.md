# HubSpot vs Salesforce: Which CRM Should You Choose in 2026?

Choosing between HubSpot and Salesforce is like choosing between a Tesla and a semi-truck. Both get you where you're going, but the experience is wildly different.

After testing both platforms with 20+ clients, here's the honest comparison nobody else will give you.

## Quick Decision Guide

**Choose HubSpot if:**
- Team size: 1-100 people
- Need marketing + sales in one platform
- Budget: Under $5,000/mo
- Want to launch in 2 weeks
- Prioritize ease of use

**Choose Salesforce if:**
- Team size: 100+ people
- Need extreme customization
- Budget: $10,000+/mo
- Have dedicated admin team
- Complex enterprise sales processes
